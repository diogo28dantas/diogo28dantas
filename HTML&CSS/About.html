I'm Diogo
, a Communication Designer currently based in Copenhagen, with a keen eye for visual concepts. Growing up in the artistic environment of Lisbon and shaped by schools and workplaces that promote inclusivity and creativity, I have become a multifaceted person with strong communication skills, valuing diversity and holding a particular interest in social and cultural projects.

With a background in Design and Business, and prior studies in Product Design, my interests extend to other fields, including interior design and furniture restoration. My primary focus lies in editorial design and shaping visual identities, with a special passion for strategy, creative writing and research.
