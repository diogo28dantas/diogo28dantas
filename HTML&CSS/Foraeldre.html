Forældre
( Visual Identity & Title Design )
2024—2025
Forældre is a sentimental short film that follows Noah, a man caring for his father suffering from dementia, as he faces unresolved grief at his ex-girlfriend’s wedding. The film delves into themes of memory, loss, and the complexity of human connection, as Noah navigates his past and lost relationships.

For this project, I was tasked with shaping the film’s visual identity, from subtitles and credits to title graphics and poster assets. The director envisioned a raw, unfiltered aesthetic that would capture the film’s reflective, intimate tone, and I made sure each graphic element aligned with this concept. Guided by the mantra, ‘what you see is what you get,’ I focused on creating visuals that enhanced the film's naturalistic style, maintaining emotional depth and authenticity.
