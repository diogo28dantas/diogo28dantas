Passionfruit
( Visual Identity & Editorial Design )
2024
Passionfruit reimagines sex education for queer individuals, addressing gaps left by traditional curricula. Focused on inclusivity, it offers a platform to explore sexuality through a queer lens, providing essential resources on topics like contraception, sexual identity, and LGBTQIA+ issues. The project includes a website, social media presence, and print materials.

As an independent project, Passionfruit was developed to empower queer individuals with accessible, evidence-based information through a bold visual identity and a multidisciplinary approach. It fosters a community-driven space for open dialogue, challenging societal norms and advocating for inclusive education. Passionfruit was exhibited at Casa do Jardim da Estrela in Lisbon from July 4th to 13th, 2024, offering a physical space to explore these vital conversations.
