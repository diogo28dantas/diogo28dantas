ABOUT
Spacon
( Visual Identity )
2024
To celebrate Spacon’s 10th anniversary, we reimagined the company’s visual identity, marking a bold new chapter in its evolution. Known for its disruptive and innovative approach in Copenhagen, the studio embraced a simplified, bolder identity, shedding its former “Spacon & X” in favor of a more streamlined, pure form of Spacon. This shift symbolized the studio’s growth and its expanded vision for the future.

Together with art director Melanie Njoh, I developed a new logo and visual identity that encapsulated the studio’s boldness and creative energy. In addition to the rebrand, Spacon embraced a new creative strategy, positioning itself as a Spatial & Visual Identity studio. The refreshed identity now communicates the studio’s expansion of services and its commitment to pioneering new models of design practice.
