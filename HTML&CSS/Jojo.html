ABOUT
Jojo
( Visual Identity )
2024—2025
JOJO, a popular bar in Vesterbro, Copenhagen, partnered with Spacon to create a completely new identity for its expansion into Nørrebro. As the brand grew, we developed a design that reflects the youthful, grounded vibe of Nørrebro, while maintaining a fluid connection with the Vesterbro location and celebrating their distinct atmospheres.

The new, organically shaped logo I designed draws inspiration from JOJO’s freshly made in-house cocktails. Working alongside art director Melanie Njoh, we ensured the identity captured both the dynamic energy and soft, inviting nature of the brand. For the Nørrebro space, masterfully designed by architects Dorsa Ali Zadeh and Aleksander Aarstad, we integrated the visual identity into Miljøparken, creating a vibrant, cozy space centered around the prominent bar concept,
