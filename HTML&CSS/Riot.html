Riot Fest
( Visual Identity & Communication )
2022
Riot Fest is a groundbreaking, multigenre festival designed to disrupt the ‘quiet’ with its diverse crowd and artist lineup. Set to unfold at Refshaleøen in Copenhagen, this open- air festival features a huge range of genres, going from Neo Soul and R&B to Hip Hop and AfroPop, creating an energetic and inclusive atmosphere.

Conceived as a fictional three-day event, Riot Fest was designed to redefine the traditional festival experience—transforming it into a loud and uncontrolled celebration of music and entertainment, reminiscent of a riot. By curating a lineup that reflects a spectrum of musical styles, the goal was to captivate a broad audience, where diversity, energy, and extravagance are at the heart of the Riot Fest experience.
