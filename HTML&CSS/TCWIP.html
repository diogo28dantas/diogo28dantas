ABOUT
The Complete Work In Progress
( Editorial & Event Design )
2024
Spacon’s The Complete Work in Progress celebrates the studio’s 10th anniversary through an experimental, hands-on editorial publication. Combining stories, experiments, and insights, this unique book breaks from tradition with its office-core inspired format—featuring reusable binders and spray-painted covers. Each element reflects the studio’s fluid, evolving creative process.

For this project, I was responsible for the editorial layout, communication materials, and event assets, including drink menus and merchandise. The publication not only captures Spacon’s design journey but also challenges the conventional approach to architecture books, celebrating the studio’s interdisciplinary philosophy and commitment to sustainability. It’s a tribute to Spacon’s dynamic, collaborative practice and a love letter to the design industry’s continuous evolution.
