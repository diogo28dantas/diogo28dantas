Cerne Jewellery

(Visual Identity)

Cerne emerged unexpectedly from a collaboration between two friends. Originally conceptualized as a hypothetical identity case, the project evolved into Margaridaâ€™s dream of starting her own jewellery business. The venture organically expanded, with a growing team working on shaping its identity and bringing the brand to life.

Positioned as an online business, Cerne embodies artisanal craftsmanship and a commitment to sustainability. With a distinctive blend of personality, playfulness, and audacity, it functions as an authorial and contemporary brand redefining the role of jewellery, elevating it from a mere accessory into a signature piece. The final identity encapsulates the bold and deliberately unfinished yet refined aesthetic of its jewels.
